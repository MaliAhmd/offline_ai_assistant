 About NI Drip Central   
 
 At NI Drip Central Electronics & Appliances, we’re proud to serve homes, landlords, and property managers across Belfast with reliable, affordable household appliances. Founded by Shawn Atkinson in 2025, the business was built around one simple idea: to make it easy for people to get quality appliances without the high retail markups or long delivery times. We supply new and professionally refurbished appliances from well-known brands, all tested for performance and backed by friendly, local service you can count on.  FAQ's   
 
 Products   
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 What products do you offer? 
 
 
 NI Drip Central Electronics & Appliances offers a comprehensive range of home appliances and electronics. We stock products from leading brands and ensure all items meet our quality standards.   
 
 
 
 Do you sell refurbished appliances? 
 
 
 Yes, we offer a carefully curated selection of refurbished appliances alongside our new products. They come with a warranty for your peace of mind.   
 
 
 
 How long does delivery take?  
 
 
 Local Belfast orders arrive in 1–3 working days, while Greater NI and ROI/UK take 3–7 days. You’ll receive full tracking upon dispatch and a personal call to book your ideal delivery slot.   
 
 
 
 What if my delivery is delayed? 
 
 
 If your delivery is delayed due to high demand or supply chain issues, we will proactively contact you to explain and reschedule at your earliest convenience.   
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Delivery & Collection   
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 What are your delivery options? 
 
 
 We offer scheduled standard delivery across Belfast, next-day express for orders placed before 2pm, and free in-store collection during business hours. Charges vary based on your location and order size   
 
 
 
 Do you deliver outside Belfast? 
 
 
 Absolutely. While Belfast is our primary service area, we provide delivery across Northern Ireland and can arrange shipping to the Republic of Ireland and mainland UK.   
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Warranty   
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 What is the warranty on New vs. Refurbished items ? 
 
 
 New products at  NI Drip Central  typically include a 1–2 year manufacturer's warranty depending on the specific brand. For our refurbished range, we provide a minimum 6-month warranty   
 
 
 
 Can I purchase extra protection for my tech? 
 
 
 Yes! We offer Extended Warranties available for purchase on selected high-value electronics and appliances.   
 
 
 
 How do I prove my warranty if I need a repair? 
 
 
 Please retain your original receipt as your primary proof of purchase for all warranty claims.Your digital receipt is also saved permanently in our system
